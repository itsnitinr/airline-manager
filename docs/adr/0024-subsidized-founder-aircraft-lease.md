# ADR-0024: Start careers with a subsidized aircraft lease

Status: accepted

## Context

Requiring a new player to understand the full aircraft and financing market
before operating a route risks a slow or confusing start. Giving the airline a
free owned narrow-body would remove a meaningful financial constraint and could
be exploited by immediately selling the asset.

## Decision

At founding, the player chooses one aircraft from a curated founder lease
package. The list includes recognizable narrow-body choices and lower-risk
regional choices capable of supporting viable starting networks.

The package is an operating lease, not ownership. Its initial deposit may be
waived or subsidized, but recurring lease payments and operating obligations
remain. The aircraft cannot be sold by the airline.

## Consequences

- Every career can reach its first route quickly.
- The starter choice communicates network and risk tradeoffs during onboarding.
- The package needs carefully balanced aircraft, lease terms, and recommended
  starter routes so each listed option is viable.
- Lease return, default, and substitution rules must prevent dead-end careers
  and resale exploits.
